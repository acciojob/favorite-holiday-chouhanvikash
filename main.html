Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It lies in the northern part of the Kullu Valley and is surrounded by towering mountains and thick forests. Manali is famous for its natural beauty, cool climate, and snow-covered peaks. People from all over India and the world come to Manali to experience its peaceful atmosphere and adventure activities. The Beas River flows through the town, adding to its scenic charm. In winter, the entire area gets covered in snow, making it look like a fairy tale land. Manali attracts tourists throughout the year, but winter and summer are the most popular seasons. It is a favorite destination for honeymooners, nature lovers, and thrill-seekers. Rohtang Pass, located near Manali, is one of the major attractions. The pass is often covered in snow and offers a breathtaking view of the surrounding mountains. Solang Valley is another famous spot, known for skiing, paragliding, and other adventure sports.
